To All Staff,
Amendment to Employee Leave Policy (Circular No. 26/2015)
This circular amends Circular No. 26/2015 effective from 1 February 2017.

e The annual leave entitlement is increased to 18 days per calendar year. Sick leave

remains at 7 days.

e Employees may now carry over up to 5 unused sick leave days to the next year.
e All other conditions of Circular No. 26/2015 remain in effect unless explicitly

modified herein.

Dileepa Wijesundera,

Group Chief Executive Officer
